ABOUT THE PROJECT

Team Grapevine partnered with the Niagara-based non-for-profit association. The Niagara Grape and Wine Festival to explore ways in which a website redesign could help the NGWF achieve increased site traffic, overall awareness, and event attendance.

APROBLEM STATEMENT

The NGWF was established in 1951 as a collective non -profit to promote the wineries and grape growers of the region with a vision to make the world “fall in love with Niagara Wine Country”.   We have observed that the NGWF website isn’t reaching an audience broad enough to capture new, or out-of-town consumers, diminishing the investment individual stakeholders (wineries) are willing to invest into the collective organization.  How might we improve the NGWF website so that new and out-of-town customers are drawn to the magic of what NGWF has to offer, bringing much needed economic support to the industry, increasing winery visibility, increasing attendance to events, and ultimately boosting the profitability of the Niagara wine region.
